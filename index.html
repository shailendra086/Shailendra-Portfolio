Key Features of AKTU Website
Online Mode

Student Services

Students can easily manage their academic tasks, like course registration, checking grades, attendance, and exam schedules. It also helps with online fee payments and exam form submissions, making things simple and quick.

Exam Management

The system streamlines the examination process by providing tools for downloading admit cards, accessing exam timetables, and viewing results. This ensures a smooth, transparent, and efficient experience for students and administrators alike.

Data Management

AKTU ERP serves as a comprehensive database that securely stores and manages institutional data. It provides quick access to details like student records, academic performance, and demographic information for the university and its affiliated colleges.

Faculty and Staff Services

Faculty can leverage the platform to share course materials, mark attendance, upload grades, and interact with students. Administrative staff benefit from features like automated payroll processing and leave management, enhancing operational efficiency.

Communication

The ERP offers robust communication tools, including notifications, announcements, and in-platform messaging. These features promote effective interaction and ensure everyone stays updated with important information.

Document Management

Students can conveniently access essential documents, such as certificates and transcripts, through the system. Faculty members can upload lecture materials, resources, and assignments, creating a centralized hub for academic resources.
